Established in 2008, Swadés is a focal point of cultural diversity at Santa Clara University, boasting an annual enrollment of 300 students and a thriving alumni network exceeding 1000 members. As one of the university's premier international student groups, Swadés transcends traditional academic roles. It serves as a vital support system, cultural conduit, and social hub, fostering enduring connections among members. Beyond the campus confines, Swadés plays a pivotal role in global outreach, promoting cultural awareness and contributing to the rich tapestry of the university community.

Swadés' impact is most evident in its ability to create a home away from home for Indian graduate students, facilitating a smooth transition into academic and social life at Santa Clara University. The extensive alumni network stands as a testament to Swadés' success in nurturing connections that endure long after members have left the campus, forming a global community with shared experiences and a deep connection to the university.
